About Me

Although I've coded since grade school I only recently decided to pursue it as a career. I bought a "be a full stack developer" bundle and, through courses of varying quality, introduced myself to Python, PHP, delved deeper into SQL, HTML, CSS, and after a few months realized I didn't know quite what to do with all this. I needed some feedback, I needed some structure, I needed a way to know I was thoroughly prepared and competitive. My research quickly lead me to LaunchSchool.

Over the course of a year I worked through a rigorous curriculum designed at every level to build and test mastery. From 'Programming Foundations' through 'DOM and Asynchronous Programming with JavaScript' I learned material to the depth required by complex, demanding projects and intense evaluations. LaunchSchool's curriculum is designed so that the former engineers who run it graduate junior engineers they would want to hire, so their students emerge armed with the tools not to use just the latest framework, but any framework, to work easily with whatever technology becomes popular over a decades-long career.

I've gained a foundation in computational thinking, an understanding of scoping, RegEx, consulting the docs, approaching problems systematically - I'm even comfortable with 'this'. After I passed the final, intense assessment, completing the LaunchSchool Core Curriculum, I found myself so intrigued by a presentation on React that I taught it to myself with Azat Mardan's excellent React Quickly, just to cover all my bases.

With a tested toolset from an excellent program I'm ready to find a great team, jump in, and get the job done. Contact me, I won't be on the market for long!
